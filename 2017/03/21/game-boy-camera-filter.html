A Game Boy Camera Filter

I first came across Claudio Gomboli's site while looking for writeups on 35mm point-and-shoots - I'm pretty sure I was looking for an opinion the Olympus Stylus Epic. Since then, I've been back to read up on others. In any case, I never expected that I'd end up spending an afternoon reading about the Game Boy Camera.

Claudio Gomboli's article about this wonderful little camera goes over its history. The camera made its debut in 1998, and at the time of its launch, it was the world's smallest digital camera.

Emulating the look of the camera wasn't particularly hard, but it did require a bit of thought. While the Game Boy Camera was made to work with a screen that could display only 4 shades, it appears to be able to capture at least 13 shades.
